FAMILY CALENDAR
Packaging DESIGN / ILLUSTRATION

designed in 2017







This is a Calendar aiming at urging people to visit their parents more often by presenting the ageing process of people in 12 different aspects.







Each day where you do not see you parents, another page of the calendar has to be added to your wall. The more often you meet your parents, the healthier and younger their images will appear on the calendar.
