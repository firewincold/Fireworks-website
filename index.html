FAMILY CALENDAR
Packaging DESIGN / ILLUSTRATION

made in 2017







This is a Calendar aim to urge people to visit their parents more often by presenting the ageing process of people in 12 espects.







Each day, a page of calendar should be put manually on the wall when you do not see your parents. The more you see your parents, the healthier or younger image would appear on the calendar.

WILDRE
PACKAGING / ILLUSTRATION / COMMUNITY

designed in 2018







Wire wild flower is a project whose aim is to save endangered wild plants on road verges in England by turning them into gardening products.







A pot of an egg shape means new life and hope. The fragile egg shell indicates that the endangered plants need to be protected.
